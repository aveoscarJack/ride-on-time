# 🚌 UniRouteX

**Smart Campus Transport. Real-Time. Reliable.**

UniRouteX is a modern smart campus shuttle management platform designed to help students travel around campus efficiently. The platform provides **live GPS tracking, real-time shuttle schedules, estimated arrival times (ETA), occupancy information, and instant notifications**, ensuring students never miss their shuttle.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://uniroutex.lovable.app/

---

# 📌 Overview

UniRouteX was developed to solve one of the most common challenges faced by university students—keeping up with shuttle schedules and unexpected route changes.

Instead of relying on notice boards, WhatsApp groups, or outdated timetables, students can simply open UniRouteX to view live shuttle information from anywhere on campus.

The platform provides a seamless experience for both students and university transport administrators through a modern, responsive interface.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Campus transportation often suffers from:

- Students missing shuttles due to schedule changes
- Long waiting times
- Poor communication between transport services and students
- No visibility of shuttle locations
- Overcrowded shuttles
- Outdated printed timetables

These issues negatively impact student attendance, punctuality, and overall campus experience.

---

# 💡 Solution

UniRouteX solves these challenges by providing a centralized platform where students can:

- Track shuttle locations live
- View updated shuttle schedules
- Receive instant notifications about delays or cancellations
- Check estimated arrival times
- See shuttle occupancy before boarding
- Access transport information directly from their student account

# 🚀 Installation

Clone the repository


Navigate into the project

